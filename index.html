Our Story

We built our business from a communal table at Starbucks. Relentlessly bootstrapping our way, we experienced the lows of financial hardship and the highs of multi-million-dollar wins. Now we’re ready to accelerate, and while we believe an Initial Coin Offer (ICO) for project funding is the right financial vehicle for us, greed has decayed the ICO market. It’s full of fraudulent projects and dishonest players — and that’s a problem for businesses like us who could benefit from it.

No one has yet to stand up to restore the health of the digital coin market. So we did.

The Problem

ICO Market is Corrupted

A lack of accountability has undermined public confidence, with no easy way to evaluate the credibility of founders and their teams.

Professional investors are still relying on “intuition” and average customers are falling prey to scams.

So What?

Why Do We Care?

Because digital token is going to be one of the most profound financial vehicles. It will empower startups and established businesses to take their MVPs directly to customers and give them vested interest, turning them into advocates. But it won’t do any of that if people can’t trust the system.

The Solution

Smash Index

Smash Index is a continuously calculated and quantifiable measurement of the value every founder and advisor brings to a company. It’s based on business achievements, market influence, and consensus evaluation from others.

Customers can gauge the likelihood of a startup’s success based on the aggregate of the teams’ Smash Indices. The more success a founder or advisor has had, the more valuable they’ll be and the more their tokens will be worth. Conversely, failures and fraudulent activity will diminish their index score and their value.

First Magic - For the Customers

A Simple Value

Customers can use Smash Index to evaluate an ICO’s intrinsic value, and the likelihood of the project’s success. The index continuously pulls information about the project, its progress and the people involved. Based on all that information, the index updates scores right away so you know objectively where a project stands now.

Second Magic - For the Entrepreneurs, Researchers, and Advisors

Reward Success, Penalize Fraud

Smash Index monitors every individual in every project they’re involved with as an entrepreneur, researcher, or advisor. Success increases their personal Smash Index scores, while failure decreases it.

Additionally, individuals participating in fraudulent projects (including advisors) will be heavily penalized with a personal reputation bankruptcy value applied to their Smash Indices.

Third Magic - For the ICO Market

Continuous Cleansing

Smash Index doesn’t just stop at ICO. It will monitor project delivery, market acceptance, revenue, and people’s successes and failures in other projects to adjust the Smash Index valuation value. Smash Index is an unstoppable force to ruthlessly cleanse the ICO market by penalizing fraudsters and vaporware talkers.

Smash Index

Business Model

We will begin Smash Index’s Initial Coin Offer on 6.16.2018 to pre-sell our Smash Index subscription to interested customers through our utility token SMSH. Customers can use SMSH as a medium to exchange for a Smash Index subscription, giving them access to real time and historic Smash Index scores for all the ICOs on the market.

Smash Index

Go-to-Market

We will use Smash Index on our own ICO as beta testing. We will use the beta model to calculate the Smash Index based on our team’s indices, including our researchers and advisors. We’ll base our initial token value on it. And the system will continuously keep track of project delivery status.

After our successful beta, we’ll make Smash Index scores available for all the available ICO projects in the marketplace so customers can start evaluating them objectively.

Epilogue

ICO market purification is step one. With Smash Index as the strong foundation valuation model, we imagine a representative index for the entire ICO market such as Smash 500 to allow customers to accurately gauge the performance of the large-cap ICO market. And we can build indices for various advanced industries such as Smash Machine Learning Index, Smash Autonomous Connected Vehicle Index, Smash Augmented Reality Index and, of course, Smash Blockchain Index where traditional indices don’t work.

When security legislation finally catches up, we can turn human capital into a tradable asset to further encourage everyone to build for success and not for scams — and the ICO market will undoubtedly become the most powerful financing platform for new products and services.
