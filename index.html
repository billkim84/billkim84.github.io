Our Story

We built our business, an institutional currency trading and analytics technology company, from a communal table at Starbucks. Relentlessly bootstrapping our way, we experienced the lows of financial hardship and the highs of multi-million-dollar wins. Now we’re ready to accelerate. While we believe an Initial Coin Offering (ICO) for project funding is the right financial vehicle for us, ICOs are still too young. There are plenty of dishonest players and customers do not have long term confidence yet — and that’s a problem for businesses like us who could benefit from it.

No one has yet to stand up to restore the health of the digital coin market. So we did.

Problem - ICO Market Still In Infancy

It suffers from a lack of accountability that undermines public confidence and offers no easy way to evaluate the credibility of founders and their teams. Professional investors are still relying on “intuition” and average customers are falling prey to scams. As a result, token values often drop sharply after the ICO and cannot serve as a long term financial vehicle.

Why Do We Care?

Because digital tokens are going to be one of the most profound financial vehicles since the evolution of the first currencies. They will empower startups and established businesses to take their MVPs directly to customers, give them a vested interest and turn them into advocates.

But it won’t do any of that if people can’t trust the system.

Towards ICO 2.0

An Independent agent, acting in the buyers’ best interest, performing continuous due diligence and providing rating and report on businesses is an effective first step towards a mature ICO market.

Solution - Smash Index

Smash Index is a continuously calculated and quantifiable measurement of the value every founder and advisor brings to a company. It’s based on business achievements, market influence, and consensus evaluation from others.

Customers can gauge the likelihood of a startup’s success based on the aggregate value of the teams’ Smash Indices. The more success a founder or advisor has had, the more valuable they’ll be and the more their tokens will be worth. Conversely, failures and fraudulent activity will diminish their index score and their value.

A Simple Valued

One number to benchmark the likelihood of the project’s success, always expressed in real-time and calculated from a carefully crafted human and social capital valuation model.

Reward Success, Penalize Fraud

Smash Index monitors every individual in every project they’re involved with as an entrepreneur, researcher or advisor. Success increases their personal Smash Index scores, while failure decreases it.

Additionally, individuals participating in fraudulent projects (including advisors) will be heavily penalized with a personal reputation bankruptcy value applied to their Smash Indices.

Continuous Improvement

Smash Index doesn’t just stop at the ICO. It will monitor project delivery, market acceptance, revenue, and people’s successes and failures in other projects to adjust the Smash Index valuation value and continuously make ICO a better market.

Business Model

We will begin Smash Index’s Initial Coin Offering on 6.16.2018 to pre-sell our Smash Index subscription to interested customers through our utility token SMSH. Customers can use SMSH as a medium of exchange for a Smash Index subscription, giving them access to real time and historic Smash Index scores for all the ICOs on the market.

Go-to-Market

Design Smash Index model and identify Input Items and the associated weight
Beta test Smash Index model by calculating Smash Index for our team members and advisors
Raise deferred revenue through our utility token
Build investigation and analytics team and technology for identity verification and forensic blockchain accounting

Epilogue

With Smash Index as the strong foundation valuation model, we imagine a representative index for the entire ICO market such as Smash 500 to allow customers to accurately gauge the performance of the large-cap ICO market. And we can build indices for various advanced industries such as Smash Machine Learning Index, Smash Autonomous Connected Vehicle Index, Smash Augmented Reality Index and, of course, Smash Blockchain Index where traditional indices don’t work.

When security legislation finally catches up, we can turn human capital into a tradable asset to further encourage everyone to build for success and not for scams — and the ICO market will undoubtedly become the most powerful financing platform for new products and services.
